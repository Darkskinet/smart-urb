Our Vision

Urban SmartFarm envisions a future where agriculture thrives in cityscapes— particularly on rooftops. We believe underutilized vertical spaces can power the next generation of sustainable food production.

Automated rooftop cultivation reimagined.

Through hydroponics, data-driven insights, and robotic automation, we bring farming to rooftops using AI-assisted monitoring — enabling consistent yield and zero soil usage.

Our goal: making fresh produce hyperlocal, sustainable, and tech-powered— even amidst skyscrapers.
